# Shaan Puri's 4 Rules of Money

> From *My First Million* (HubSpot Media). This is the actual playbook Shaan used to go from broke to $1M at age 30, and $25M a few years later.

---

## Rule 1: Master ONE Money-Making Skill

There are only 4 real money-making skills:

| Skill | What It Means |
|-------|--------------|
| **Selling** | Persuading, marketing, closing deals |
| **Making** | Creating products, apps, videos, books |
| **Designing** | Taste, form and function (Steve Jobs style) |
| **Hunting** | Spotting opportunities, deals, investments |

Pick ONE. Master it. You don't need to be in the top 1% — just the top 20%.

**The MrBeast method:** He decided at 12 his skill would be making YouTube videos. Every day he'd flip to random dictionary words and force himself to come up with 20 video ideas per word. He tried to improve one thing each day — thumbnail, lighting, sound. 150+ videos before 10k subscribers. Now the biggest YouTuber on the planet.

**The Buffett method:** Warren Buffett read the entire Moody's Manual — 4,000 pages of corporate financials — cover to cover, hunting for mispriced stocks. He credits his career on only 12 good investing decisions.

**Bottom line:** Master one skill = path to millions. Master two = path to billions.

---

## Rule 2: Don't Rent Your Time. Own Equity.

Most people take their skill and get *hired* for it. They climb the corporate ladder, get raises, think they're winning.

That's the wrong direction.

> "The two most addictive things in the world are heroin and a monthly salary." — Nassim Taleb

**Two ways to own equity:**
1. Start a business
2. Invest in one

**Three vehicles to turn skill into equity:**

| Vehicle | How It Works | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Code** | Build products, apps, software that scale | Designer quit $200k job, built Shopify themes, now $2M/year selling to 2,000 customers while he sleeps |
| **Content** | Create videos, books, courses | Alex Hormozi turned his selling skill into content — last book launch: 3M copies, $100M in one weekend |
| **Capital** | Use your skill to invest | Cousin spent 2 years learning multifamily real estate hunting — first year: $40M in deals with other people's money |

**Bottom line:** If you only rent your time, you'll never escape the treadmill. Equity is your only exit.

---

## Rule 3: Be Impatient With Action, Patient With Results

You don't plant a seed and dig it up the next day screaming "Why aren't you growing?!"

If you follow rules 1 and 2, getting rich is inevitable. But inevitable ≠ instantaneous.

**The secret:** Move fast on execution. Wait patiently for results.

Bad news: It takes trial and error. Can take years.  
Good news: You only need to get rich once in your lifetime.

**The Steve Martin method:** He decided to practice banjo for 40 years until he got good. He didn't need 40 — within a decade he won multiple Grammys. His persistence made it unreasonable for him to NOT succeed.

---

## Rule 4: Proximity Is Power

Good things take time — but you can speed it up.

How? **Move.** Literally.

- Want to be in tech? Move to San Francisco.
- Want to make movies? Move to LA.
- Want to build wealth? Get around people who are doing it.

> "If you want to change your life, change your zip code." — Shaan Puri

You learn through osmosis. Hang around broke complainers, you become one. Hang around builders, you level up automatically.

At 24, Shaan was in Australia, started a company, went to a founder meetup, got asked to give the keynote. He wasn't flattered — he was horrified. *If I'm the smartest guy in the room, I'm in the wrong room.* He bought a one-way ticket to San Francisco the next week.

## How the 5 Skills Map to These Rules

| Skill | Primary Rule 1 Category | Rule 2 Equity Vehicle | Rule 4 Community |
|-------|------------------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Copywriting | **Selling** + Making | Content (courses, newsletter) or Code (SaaS copy tool) | Twitter copywriters, direct response communities |
| Web Development | **Making** + Designing | Code (templates, SaaS, client productized service) | GitHub, Webflow forums, Indie Hackers |
| Video Editing | **Making** + Designing | Content (YouTube channel) or Code (editing presets/LUTs) | YouTube creator communities, motion design Discord |
| Email Marketing | **Selling** | Code (email SaaS) or Content (newsletter audience) | ConvertKit/Klaviyo communities, email marketers on Twitter |
| Prompt Engineering | **Making** + Hunting | Code (AI tools, automations) or Capital (early AI investment) | AI builders on Twitter/X, LLM Discord servers |

---

## What Happens When You Follow All 4 Rules

1. You master a money-making skill
2. You turn that skill into equity (not a job)
3. You stay patient while taking massive action
4. You move closer to where the action is

Result: You flip the odds from 90% failure to 90% success.

> "The best advice in the world is to take actions that make it unreasonable for you to fail." — Shaan Puri
