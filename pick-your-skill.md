# Pick Your Skill

Not sure which of the 5 skills to pursue? Answer these 4 questions.

---

## Question 1: What's Your Income Goal in 12 Months?

| Goal | Best Fit |
|------|----------|
| First $1k–$3k/month freelancing | Copywriting or Email Marketing (fastest to first dollar) |
| $5k–$10k/month freelancing | Web Development or Video Editing |
| $10k+/month or employed full-time | Prompt Engineering or Web Development |
| Build an audience + eventually sell | Copywriting or Video Editing |

---

## Question 2: What's Your Personality?

| You are... | Best Fit |
|------------|----------|
| A natural talker / persuader | Copywriting |
| A builder who loves making things | Web Development |
| Obsessed with storytelling and visuals | Video Editing |
| Detail-oriented, analytical | Email Marketing |
| Curious about technology and systems | Prompt Engineering |

---

## Question 3: How Much Time Do You Have Per Day?

| Time Available | Best Fit |
|---------------|----------|
| 30–45 min/day | Copywriting or Prompt Engineering (shortest path to proficiency) |
| 60–90 min/day | Email Marketing or Video Editing |
| 2+ hours/day | Web Development (needs more hands-on build time) |

---

## Question 4: What Type of Work Energizes You?

| You love... | Best Fit |
|-------------|----------|
| Writing and persuasion | Copywriting or Email Marketing |
| Building visual things people see | Web Development or Video Editing |
| Systems, automation, leverage | Prompt Engineering |
| Seeing measurable ROI for clients | Email Marketing (best documented ROI: $35–$45 per $1 spent) |

---

## Quick Picks

**Fastest to first dollar:** Copywriting  
**Highest ceiling (2026+):** Prompt Engineering  
**Best for introverts:** Email Marketing or Web Development  
**Best for creators / content builders:** Video Editing or Copywriting  
**Best Shaan Rule 2 path to equity:** Web Development (Code) or Copywriting (Content)

---

## Shaan's Advice on Picking

> "The question isn't 'can I learn these?' — it's 'which one fits MY situation?' Pick the wrong one and you waste 6 months. Pick the right one and you change the next 10 years."
