## Shaan's Framework: Which Categories This Hits

| Category | How Email Marketing Fits |
|----------|-------------------------|
| **Selling** (primary) | Email is a direct-response selling channel — every sequence is engineered to convert |
| **Making** (secondary) | You produce sequences, automations, and campaigns — assets that keep selling |

This is the most underrated **Selling** skill. Most people chase social media while ignoring email — which has a 40x higher ROI than social media. That gap is your opportunity.

---

## Why Most People Overlook This Skill

- It's not as "visible" as copywriting or video
- It feels technical (automations, segmentation, deliverability)
- The results are buried in dashboards, not on public feeds

That invisibility is the advantage. There's less competition, higher retention (clients keep you for months/years), and businesses are desperate for people who actually understand it.

---

## Shaan's Rule 2: How Email Marketing Becomes Equity

| Vehicle | What It Looks Like |
|---------|--------------------|
| **Content** | Build your own newsletter audience — that list is an asset you own |
| **Code** | Build email automation templates, productized service, or email SaaS tool |
| **Capital** | Buy underperforming email lists / businesses and fix their email program |

The content play here is massive: a newsletter with 10,000 engaged subscribers is worth more than most people realize. Businesses will pay you to be featured. Sponsors pay $500–$5,000 per email blast. And you own the audience — no algorithm, no platform risk.

## What $5,000/Month Email Marketers Do Differently

1. They think in systems — not just individual emails, but full lifecycle flows
2. They obsess over subject lines (50% of success is getting the open)
3. They segment ruthlessly — the right message to the right person beats broadcast every time
4. They track metrics that matter: revenue per email, not just open rate
5. They understand deliverability — an email that lands in spam is worth zero
