## Phase 1 — Foundation (Days 1–30)

### Week 1–2: Email Psychology + Deliverability

---

### Day 1 — Why Email Still Wins
**Task:** Read the DMA's email marketing benchmark report and 3 articles on email ROI. Write a one-page summary of why email outperforms social: ownership, deliverability, ROI data ($36–$45 return per $1 spent), and no algorithm risk.

**Posts:**

### Day 2 — How Email Works Technically
**Task:** Learn how email actually travels from sender to inbox: SMTP, DNS records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), mail servers, and inbox providers. Set up a free SendGrid or Mailgun account to see the technical side. Understanding deliverability starts here.

**Posts:**
