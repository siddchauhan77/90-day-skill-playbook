# Video Editing: 90-Day Plan

## Phase Overview
- Phase 1 — Foundation (Days 1–30): DaVinci Resolve interface, cuts, transitions, first edit
- Phase 2 — Application (Days 31–60): Color grading, sound design, YouTube-style edits, short-form
- Phase 3 — Monetization (Days 61–90): Portfolio reel, outreach to creators, first paid gig

> Primary tool: DaVinci Resolve (free). Download at blackmagicdesign.com.
> Free footage for practice: pexels.com/videos and mixkit.co

> One rule: edit something every single day — even if it's 2 minutes of raw footage. Volume creates taste.
