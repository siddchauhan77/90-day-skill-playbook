## Shaan's Framework: Which Categories This Hits

| Category | How Copywriting Fits |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Selling** (primary) | Copywriting IS persuasion. Every word is designed to move someone from uninterested to action. |
| **Making** (secondary) | You produce written assets — sales pages, emails, ads, landing pages — that live and work 24/7 |

This is one of the most pure **Selling** skills on the list. If you learn to write words that make people buy, you will never be broke.

## Shaan's Rule 2: How Copywriting Becomes Equity

| Vehicle | What It Looks Like |
|---------|--------------------|
| **Content** | Build a newsletter, Twitter audience, or course around copywriting — Alex Hormozi style |
| **Code** | Build a productized copy service or SaaS tool (headline generators, email sequence builders) |
| **Capital** | Use selling skills to raise money for your own projects |

Most copywriters stop at freelancing — that's renting your time. The real play: write copy for your OWN product or audience. Then you own the upside.

---

## Why the Bar to Stand Out Is Low

Most copywriters are terrible. They use:
- Vague claims nobody believes
- Passive, lifeless language
- Features instead of outcomes
- Weak hooks that lose readers in the first line

You don't have to be Gary Bencivenga. You just have to be better than 80% of people writing copy right now. That's achievable in 90 days.

## What $250/hour Copywriters Do Differently

1. They lead with the reader's pain, not the product's features
2. Their headline makes a specific, believable promise
3. Every paragraph earns the next one
4. They write conversationally — no corporate jargon
5. They test constantly and let data decide what works
