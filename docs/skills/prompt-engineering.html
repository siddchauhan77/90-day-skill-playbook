How to sell this skill

The pitch, the proof, what you hand over, and what the client actually measures.

Your pitch

"I'll build you one AI workflow this week — free. If it saves your team 5+ hours, we talk about what else needs automating."

Proof that closes
  • Loom demo of a working automation (timestamped, shows before/after)
  • Hours-saved calculation: task time before vs. after
  • ROI case study: hours × hourly rate = $X saved per month
  • GitHub or Notion with a public prompt library you maintain
What you deliver
  • Working prompt or workflow (fully documented)
  • System prompt library (versioned)

Fastest path to first client

No cold blast. No waiting 60 days. Here's the exact sequence to land client #1.

First client in ~3–7 days
  1. Day 1: Post on LinkedIn or Twitter: "I'll build 3 AI workflows for free for 5 businesses this week. DM me what's eating your time." AI automation demand is live and unsatisfied — you will get DMs.
  2. Days 2–3: Find solopreneurs on Twitter who post about being overwhelmed with admin work. DM them with a specific workflow you'd automate for their exact situation. Show you already thought it through.
  3. Days 4–5: Join Make.com's community, n8n Discord, and AI automation Facebook groups. Answer questions. People who get help from you publicly will pay you privately.
  4. Days 6–7: Offer a free 30-min "AI Workflow Audit" call to any business owner in your network. On the call, identify 3 tasks they could automate. Deliver 1 for free. Charge for the rest.
⚡ This is the fastest-to-client skill on this list because most businesses have never talked to anyone about AI automation. You have almost no competition in direct outreach.
