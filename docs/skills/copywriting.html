Why this skill

Copywriting is the only skill on this list that directly makes sales. Not indirectly — directly. You write words, those words generate revenue, and businesses will pay well for that. Every business that sells anything needs copy: landing pages, emails, ads, product descriptions, sales decks. The demand is everywhere.

The equity path is real too. Good copywriters build audiences by sharing their work publicly. That audience becomes an asset — newsletter sponsorships, consulting clients, courses, and SaaS tools built on your own audience.

How to sell this skill

The pitch, the proof, what you hand over, and what the client actually measures.

Your pitch

"I'll rewrite your landing page (or welcome sequence, or ad). If the new version doesn't outperform the old one in 30 days, you don't pay."

Proof that closes
  • Before/after rewrite of a real brand's page — annotated with why
  • Teardown of a known brand's copy, posted publicly
  • 1 real result: conversion lift %, email open rate improvement
  • Spec portfolio: 3 rewrites for brands you want to work with
What you deliver
  • Copy doc (Google Doc, versioned)
  • 3 headline/hook variations per section
  • Mobile-optimized language notes
  • Optional: Loom walkthrough of every decision made
Outcomes to promise
  • Conversion rate lift (%)
  • Email open + click rate
  • Revenue per visitor
  • Cost per acquisition drop
  • Time-on-page (engagement signal)
