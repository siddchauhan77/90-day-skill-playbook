Why this skill

Most people chase social media while ignoring email — which has a 40x higher ROI. That gap is your opportunity. Email is direct, owned, and algorithm-proof. Businesses that understand this pay well and keep you on long-term retainers. The invisibility of this skill is the advantage: less competition, higher retention, and desperate demand from businesses that know they need it but don't have the expertise.

How to sell this skill

The pitch, the proof, what you hand over, and what the client actually measures.

Your pitch

"I'll audit your welcome sequence for free and show you exactly what's costing you opens and revenue. If you want me to fix it, we talk retainer."

Proof that closes
  • Free audit PDF of a real brand — sent cold as outreach
  • Before/after: open rate and click rate from a sequence you rewrote
  • Spec work: 5-email welcome sequence for a brand you want to work with
  • 1 revenue attribution story: email sent → sales generated
What you deliver
  • Full email copy doc (every email, subject lines + preview text)
  • Klaviyo or ConvertKit setup + automation flow
  • Segmentation logic doc
  • 3 subject line variants per email
  • Monthly performance report template
Outcomes to promise
  • Open rate (benchmark: 20–25% for most industries)
  • Click-through rate
  • Revenue per email sent
  • List growth rate (monthly)
  • Unsubscribe rate (should trend down)

Build for yourself first

Do the thing for yourself before you sell it to others. The output becomes your proof of work.

Start your own newsletter

Pick a niche. Grow it publicly. Share your open rates + growth milestones. A 500-subscriber list with 45% open rates is better proof than any claim.

Proof → Open rate screenshots + growth chart

Build your own welcome sequence

Write a 5-email sequence for your own newsletter or offer. Document it. Show open rates per email. This is the exact product you're selling.

Proof → Performance data per email

Free public audit of a known brand

Pick a brand's welcome sequence (subscribe to it). Write a 1-page audit of what's wrong and how to fix it. Cold email it to their CMO. Post it publicly.

Proof → Audit PDF + cold email reply

Klaviyo flow for a real Shopify store

Offer to set up flows for a small Shopify brand at cost or free. Screenshot the flow map and revenue attribution after 30 days.

Proof → Flow screenshot + revenue data
