Email Marketing

The most underrated skill on this list. Businesses know email ROI is $35–$45 per $1 spent — and they'll pay well for someone who can deliver it.

$60k–$110k/yr employed · $1.5k–$5k/mo per client · First client possible by Day 45

Why this skill

Most people chase social media while ignoring email — which has a 40x higher ROI. That gap is your opportunity. Email is direct, owned, and algorithm-proof. Businesses that understand this pay well and keep you on long-term retainers. The invisibility of this skill is the advantage: less competition, higher retention, and desperate demand from businesses that know they need it but don't have the expertise.

How it becomes equity

Content

Build your own newsletter audience. A list of 10,000 engaged subscribers is worth more than most people realize. Sponsors pay $500–$5,000 per send.

Code

Build email automation templates, a productized service, or an email SaaS tool. Package your systems into products.

Capital

Buy underperforming email lists and businesses, fix their email program, and flip or grow them.

The 90-day path

Days 1–30
Foundation

Email psychology, deliverability, welcome sequences, AIDA applied to email. Set up Klaviyo and ConvertKit accounts.

Days 31–60
Application

Nurture sequences, sales emails, abandoned cart flows, platform mastery. Audit real brand email programs.

Days 61–90
Monetization

Case study, cold outreach, first retainer client. Target e-commerce brands on Shopify or creator newsletters.

Platform fluency you need

  • Klaviyo E-commerce (Shopify brands) — highest market demand, learn this first
  • ConvertKit Creators, coaches, solopreneurs — great for newsletters
  • Mailchimp Small businesses, nonprofits — good for basic automation
  • Study Subscribe to 20 email lists. Analyze every welcome sequence you receive.
  • Swipe File Really Good Emails (reallygoodemails.com) — the best email inspiration resource
