The Framework

Master ONE skill

Selling, Making, Designing, or Hunting. Pick one. Go deep. Breadth comes after depth.

Rule 02

Don't rent time — own equity

Turn your skill into Code (products), Content (audience), or Capital (investments).

Impatient with action

Move fast on execution. Be patient with results. Compounding takes time.

Proximity is power

Get around people already doing what you want to do. Environment beats willpower.

Here's an actual day from the Copywriting track.

Day 14 — Copywriting

Headlines That Demand Attention

Task

Write 25 headlines for the same product using 5 different formulas. Compare: curiosity gap, specific benefit, "how to," contrarian, and fear of loss. Rate each 1–5. Identify your top formula and your weakest. The goal isn't one great headline — it's understanding why each formula works.

Posts — pick any
1
"I wrote 25 headlines for the same product today. Here's the one formula that dominated." — share the comparison table + your winning formula with examples
2
"Most copywriters use 1 headline formula. The best ones rotate between 5. Here's the breakdown." — 5 formula breakdown with an example of each
3
"The headline formula that feels 'too salesy' is also the one that converts the most. Here's why." — contrarian take on fear-of-loss headlines, with data on why they work

About This Project

Built by Sidd Chauhan

The thinking behind it — for case studies, portfolios, and interviews.

The Problem

Most skill-learning resources give you a curriculum but no daily execution plan. And none of them help you build in public at the same time — so learning never compounds into an audience or clients.

The Approach

Applied Shaan Puri's 4 Rules of Money as the organizing framework. Structured each skill into Foundation → Application → Monetization phases. Every day: one task, one post idea, one hook.

The Insight

Most learning resources stop at "what to learn." This specifies what to practice AND what to post about it — so learning becomes a public compounding asset, not a private hobby.

The Output

5 complete 90-day curricula. 450 daily entries. Every post idea has the exact opening hook line written out — no blank page, no excuses.
