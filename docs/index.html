Day 14 — Copywriting

Headlines That Demand Attention

Task

Write 25 headlines for the same product using 5 different formulas. Compare: curiosity gap, specific benefit, "how to," contrarian, and fear of loss. Rate each 1–5. Identify your top formula and your weakest.

Posts — pick any
1
"I wrote 25 headlines for the same product today. Here's the one formula that dominated." — share the comparison table + winning formula
2
"Most copywriters use 1 headline formula. The best ones rotate between 5. Here's the breakdown." — 5 formula breakdown with examples
3
"The headline formula that feels 'too salesy' is also the one that converts most. Here's why." — contrarian take on fear-of-loss with data
