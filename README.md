# 90-Day Skill Playbook

> Master one high-income skill in 90 days. Build in public every day. Turn it into equity — not just a job.

This repo combines **Shaan Puri's 4 Rules of Money** with a practical 90-day roadmap for 5 of the highest-demand skills available right now. Every day has a specific practice task and 1–3 ready-to-post content ideas so you never stall on "what do I work on" or "what do I post."

---

## The Framework: Shaan's 4 Rules of Money

From the *My First Million* podcast. The condensed version:

1. **Master ONE money-making skill** — Selling, Making, Designing, or Hunting. Pick one. Go deep.
2. **Don't rent your time. Own equity.** — Turn your skill into Code (products), Content (courses/audience), or Capital (investing).
3. **Be impatient with action, patient with results** — Move fast on execution. Wait for the compounding.
4. **Proximity is power** — Get around people who are already doing what you want to do.

→ Full breakdown: [`framework/shaan-4-rules.md`](framework/shaan-4-rules.md)

## How to Use This Repo

1. **Pick your skill** — use [`pick-your-skill.md`](pick-your-skill.md) if you're undecided
2. **Read the skill overview** — each skill folder has a `README.md` with income data, Shaan category mapping, and equity path
3. **Open the 90-day plan** — follow it day by day. Do the task. Post one of the content ideas.
4. **Build in public** — the post ideas are already written. No excuses.
