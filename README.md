This repo combines **Shaan Puri's 4 Rules of Money** with a practical 90-day roadmap for 5 of the highest-demand skills available right now.

Every day has:
- A **specific 30–60 min practice task** so you always know what to work on
- **1–3 ready-to-post content ideas** with exact hook lines so you never stall on "what do I post"

No vague instructions. No "practice today." Everything is written out.

## The Framework: Shaan's 4 Rules of Money

*From the My First Million podcast.*

| # | Rule | The Idea |
|---|------|----------|
| 1 | **Master ONE money-making skill** | Selling, Making, Designing, or Hunting. Pick one. Go deep. |
| 2 | **Don't rent your time — own equity** | Turn your skill into Code (products), Content (courses/audience), or Capital (investing). |
| 3 | **Be impatient with action, patient with results** | Move fast on execution. Wait for the compounding. |
| 4 | **Proximity is power** | Get around people already doing what you want to do. |

→ Full breakdown with skill mapping: [`framework/shaan-4-rules.md`](framework/shaan-4-rules.md)

## How to Use This Repo

**Step 1 — Fork it.** This is your personal playbook.

**Step 2 — Pick a skill.** Use `pick-your-skill.md` if you're undecided.

**Step 3 — Read the skill README.** Each one covers: income ranges, which of Shaan's categories it hits, how it becomes equity, and top free resources to start.

**Step 4 — Open the 90-day plan.** Follow it day by day. Do the task. Post one of the content ideas. The hooks are already written — just open your notes app and go.

**Step 5 — Build in public.** Post one thing every day. The point isn't perfection. It's compounding evidence that you're doing the work.

## What a Daily Entry Looks Like

```
### Day 14 — Headlines That Demand Attention

Task: Write 25 headlines for the same product using 5 different formulas.
Compare: curiosity gap, specific benefit, "how to," contrarian, and fear of loss.

Posts:
1. Hook: "I wrote 25 headlines for the same product today. Here's the one formula that dominated." 
   — angle: share the comparison table + winning formula
2. Hook: "Most copywriters use 1 headline formula. The best ones rotate between 5. Here's the breakdown."
   — angle: 5 formula breakdown with examples
```

Every task is specific. Every hook is a real opening line you can post today.
